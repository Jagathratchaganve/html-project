ABOUT ST

ST Corporation is an American multinational chain of coffeehouses and roastery reserves headquartered in Seattle, Washington. It is the world's largest coffeehouse chain. As of October 2021, the company had 33,833 stores in 80 countries, 15,444 of which were located in the United States.

Starbucks Trivia

When did Starbucks first open its store?

According to official Starbucks records, the first Starbucks store opened in Seattle’s Pike Place Market in 1971. (Source: Starbucks Stories & News )
